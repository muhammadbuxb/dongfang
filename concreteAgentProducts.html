Expansion Agent

Expansion Agent The expansion agent is mainly used in the material production,and the more common is the concrete expansion agent and the refractory material expansion agent. In the process of hardening of concrete and amorphous refractory materials, it is often caused by the shrinkage of the raw material itself. The expansion agent is used to introduce quantitative volume expansion and compensate the shrinkage value of the material itself, preventing the material from contracting and cracking and affecting its structure, function and appearance. In recent years, with the extensive research and development of composite materials, the expansion materials have been widely applied to the production and manufacturing of materials.
